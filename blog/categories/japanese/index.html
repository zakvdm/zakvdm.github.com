About zakvdm.org

This is the personal blog of Zak van der Merwe. I write about about Japanese, Computer Science, technology and, well, whatever else comes to mind.

I'm a computer programmer living in Cape Town, South Africa. I work for Amazon on their cloud computing platform. In my mid 20s, I lived in Japan for a couple of years, and I've had an interest in the country and the language ever since.

You can select a category below if you're looking for content on a specific topic. Or subscribe to all of them if you happen to have the exact same interests as me - in which case, you might also be interested in being my friend??
